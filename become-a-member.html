WHY SHOULD YOU BECOME A MEMBER?

Your membership in the Chamber of Commerce makes you part of a great organization that balances networking and educational opportunities for business owners with fun community projects. Membership levels are based on number of employees and type of business.
Membership benefits include:
  • Exclusive invitation to ribbon cuttings, workshops, and programs like Business After Hours, SMART Talks, Bond County Business Network Luncheons, Shop Local events, and Behind the Scenes.
  • Annual programs like the Gerald Turley Award, New Faces & Places, and Cardinals Baseball Lunch.
  • Volunteer opportunities at events like the Labor Day Golf Tournament, and Come Home For Christmas Event.
  • Membership plaque and window cling.
  • Business listing on Chamber website.
